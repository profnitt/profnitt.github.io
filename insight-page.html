Insight

Every year, a distinguished alumni in the field of finance is invited to interact with the student community and put forward their opinions on various upcoming domains of finance.

Past Events

2 . Interactive Q&A session with Prasanna Mathiannal, Head Trader at Standard Chartered Bank, Hong Kong, who talked about the realities of trading.

3 . Case Solving Competitions: The club also conducts various case-solving competitions throughout the year, where students try solving real-life industry problems.
